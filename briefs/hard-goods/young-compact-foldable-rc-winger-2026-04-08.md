## PRODUCT OVERVIEW

The Compact Foldable RC Winger is a remote-controlled bird launcher and dummy thrower engineered from the ground up for retriever sports trainers who are done making trade-offs between portability and performance. Every winger on the market today folds flat - producing long, awkward configurations that require a truck bed or ATV to move across a training field. This product eliminates that problem entirely by replacing the flat-fold with a camp-chair-style collapsing mechanism that packs the fully functional winger into a clean rectangular carry bag at a target weight of 8 pounds or less - less than two-thirds of the lightest competitor currently available. Compatible with Garmin and Dog Truck OEM electronic trigger systems, capable of launching a 3-3.5 lb duck or training dummy from 25 to 300 yards, and built to stand up to mud, water, and repeated field cycles, this winger is designed for the trainer who wants to carry three setups into the field on their own - no four-wheeler required.

## OBJECTIVES

FOLDING MECHANISM AND STRUCTURAL ARCHITECTURE
- Design a camp-chair-style folding mechanism that collapses the winger into a compact rectangular envelope suitable for a standard carry bag
- Engineer fold geometry, pivot points, and latch/lock system to withstand repeated launch-force cycles under field conditions
- Validate that the deployed configuration and folded envelope can coexist within a target weight of 8 lbs or less

THROW ARM AND LAUNCH SYSTEM
- Design a throw arm capable of consistently launching a 3-3.5 lb duck or training dummy from 25 to 300 yards
- Ensure the throw arm integrates with the folding structure without requiring disassembly between deployed and stowed positions
- Evaluate structural performance of the throw arm and pivot under repeated load cycles

ELECTRONICS INTERFACE HOUSING
- Design mechanical mounting provisions and housing to accommodate client-supplied Garmin and Dog Truck OEM electronic trigger modules
- Ensure wire routing and cable management are compatible with the folding structure - no pinch points, no exposed runs at pivot locations
- Confirm weatherproof access and sealed electronics compartment specification

MATERIALS AND WEATHERPROOFING
- Explore material strategy to achieve the 8 lb or less weight target while meeting rust-resistance and weatherproofing requirements for sustained outdoor field use
- Develop CMF direction that communicates field durability and sport-specific credibility
- Confirm hardware and fastener specifications appropriate for corrosive outdoor environments

HERO SKU AND FUTURE SCALABILITY ARCHITECTURE
- Design the hero SKU as the primary product while establishing a modular structural architecture that can accommodate 2-3 size or force-tier variants in future development phases
- Evaluate scalability provisions so future SKU additions do not require full structural redesign

## RED FLAGS

| Flag | Severity | Action Required |
|---|---|---|
| Novel Multi-System Mechanism | high | Center-fold mechanism integrated with force dynamics is genuinely novel in this product category. No competitor has attempted this. Product team hours reflect this complexity. |
| IP/Patent Risk | high | Client directly asked about patents on competing designs. Exclude patent search and IP analysis from scope - refer to IP attorney. Do not reference competing products by name in deliverables. |
| Future Product Ecosystem | medium | Client discussed small, medium, and large SKU pack, carrying cases, wagons, and accessories. This brief covers single hero SKU prototype only. Future ecosystem is a separate engagement. |
| Multi-SKU Portfolio Scope | medium | Multiple size variants discussed. Confirm single hero SKU scope for this phase with client before kickoff. Scalability architecture included in objectives but not full multi-SKU design. |
| Electronics Housing | medium | Product must accommodate standard third-party servo trigger systems (Garmin, DogTruck). Mechanical housing interface is in scope; electrical engineering is excluded. |
