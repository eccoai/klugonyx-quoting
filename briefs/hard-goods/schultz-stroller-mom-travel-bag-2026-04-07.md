## PRODUCT OVERVIEW

The Stroller Mom Travel Bag is a line of padded, protective stroller travel bags built for parents who have invested in premium strollers and need a serious solution for protecting them in transit. Every bag on the market today either comes from the stroller brand itself - making it useless for parents with a different stroller - or is a low-quality Amazon universal option with minimal padding and no brand credibility. The Stroller Mom Travel Bag fills that gap with a properly padded, durable, fashion-forward bag designed to fit a wider range of strollers than any current option on the market. Launching with two hero SKUs - a side-by-side double stroller bag and a single and travel stroller bag - this product is purpose-built for the parent who spent $500 to $1,500 on their stroller and expects the same quality standard from the accessories they use to protect it. The side-by-side SKU targets an entirely unserved need: a wheeled travel bag for double strollers, where parents are currently hauling 38 pounds of stroller through an airport with no rolling support.

## OBJECTIVES

UNIVERSAL FIT AND SIZING ARCHITECTURE
- Design a bag geometry and closure system that fits a wider range of stroller brands and models than current single-brand options
- Establish dimensional specifications for the side-by-side SKU and the single/travel SKU that cover the primary strollers in each category
- Validate that universal fit does not compromise a snug, secure pack profile

STRUCTURE, PADDING, AND PROTECTION
- Design an interior padding system that protects premium strollers from airline handling damage
- Specify padding thickness, density, and placement zones based on stroller vulnerability points
- Ensure the padded structure maintains bag integrity under repeated airline gate-check and cargo handling conditions

MOBILITY AND CARRY SYSTEM (SIDE-BY-SIDE SKU)
- Design a wheel and handle system that allows parents to roll a 35-40 lb loaded bag through an airport
- Evaluate wheel placement, durability, and load requirements for a bag carrying full stroller weight
- Ensure the carry and roll system is compatible with the bag's storage configuration when not in use

PACKABILITY AND STORAGE
- Design the bag to compress or fold down for home storage between trips
- Evaluate how packability requirements interact with the padding structure and overall bag rigidity
- Confirm a storage configuration that is practical for parents with limited closet space

CMF AND BRAND DIRECTION
- Develop a color, material, and finish strategy that positions the product as premium and modern within the baby gear category
- Design in black as the primary colorway with branded color elements at handles, zippers, and trim reflecting The Stroller Mom brand identity
- Ensure the finished product reads as a designed, branded bag - not a commodity import

MULTI-SKU ARCHITECTURE
- Design the side-by-side double SKU and the single/travel SKU within a shared design language and construction system
- Establish a structural and materials framework that allows both SKUs to share components and manufacturing processes where possible
