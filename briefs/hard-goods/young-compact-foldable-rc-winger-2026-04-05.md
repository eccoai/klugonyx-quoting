## PRODUCT OVERVIEW

The Compact Foldable RC Winger is a remote-controlled bird launcher and dummy thrower designed from the ground up for retriever sports trainers who are done compromising portability for performance. Every winger currently on the market folds flat — producing long, awkward configurations that demand a truck bed or ATV to haul across a training field. This product rejects that form factor entirely, replacing the flat-fold with a camp-chair-style collapsing mechanism that packs the fully functional winger into a clean rectangular carry bag at a target weight of 8 pounds or less — less than two-thirds of the lightest competitor on the market. Compatible with Garmin and Dog Truck OEM electronic trigger systems, capable of launching a 3–3.5 lb duck or training dummy at distances from 25 to 300 yards, and engineered to hold up against mud, water, and repeated field cycles, this winger is purpose-built for the trainer who wants to carry three setups into the field on their own — without a four-wheeler.

## OBJECTIVES

FOLDING MECHANISM & STRUCTURAL ARCHITECTURE
- Design a camp-chair-style folding mechanism that collapses the winger into a compact rectangular packed envelope suitable for a standard carry bag
- Engineer fold geometry, pivot points, and latch/lock system to withstand repeated launch-force cycles under field conditions
- Validate that deployed configuration and folded envelope can coexist within a target weight of 8 lbs or less

THROW ARM & LAUNCH SYSTEM
- Design a throw arm capable of consistently launching a 3–3.5 lb duck or training dummy from 25 to 300 yards
- Ensure throw arm integrates with the folding structure without requiring disassembly between deployed and stowed positions
- Evaluate structural performance of throw arm and pivot under repeated load cycles

ELECTRONICS INTERFACE HOUSING
- Design mechanical mounting provisions and housing to accommodate client-supplied Garmin and Dog Truck OEM electronic trigger modules
- Ensure wire routing and cable management are compatible with the folding structure — no pinch points, no exposed runs at pivot locations
- Confirm weatherproof access and sealed electronics compartment specification

MATERIALS & WEATHERPROOFING
- Explore material strategy to achieve the ≤8 lb weight target while meeting rust-resistance and weatherproofing requirements for sustained outdoor field use
- Develop CMF direction communicating field durability and sport-specific credibility
- Confirm hardware and fastener specifications appropriate for corrosive outdoor environments

HERO SKU & FUTURE SCALABILITY ARCHITECTURE
- Design hero SKU as the primary product while establishing a modular structural architecture that can accommodate 2–3 size or force-tier variants in future development phases
- Evaluate scalability provisions so future SKU additions do not require full structural redesign
